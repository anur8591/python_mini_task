# 🌟 Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to creating a welcoming, friendly, and respectful environment for everyone.

Everyone is welcome regardless of experience level, background, or identity.

---

## Our Standards

Examples of positive behavior include:

- Being respectful
- Helping beginners
- Giving constructive feedback
- Being patient and kind
- Encouraging learning

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment or discrimination
- Offensive language
- Personal attacks
- Spam
- Trolling

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers have the right to remove comments, issues, or pull requests that do not follow this Code of Conduct.

---

## Be Respectful ❤️

This project exists to help people learn.

Let's build a welcoming community where everyone feels comfortable asking questions and contributing.

Happy Coding! 🚀
