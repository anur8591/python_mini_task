***************_1.Rent_Calculator_***************
summery:
    Suppose you live in a hostel or apartment and share your room with some colleagues or friends. 
    You all share common accommodation bills and expenses that everyone needs to contribute toward. 
    This program is designed to calculate the total expenses and divide them into equal parts 
    so everyone can contribute equally.

inputs = 
    The inputs we need from the user are:
        * Total rent
        * Total food ordered for snacking
        * Electricity units spent
        * Charge per unit
        * Number of persons living in the room

output =
    * Total amount to be paid overall
    * Total amount each person needs to pay

Workflow of Project
1.input form user(Rock,Paper,Scissor)
2.Computer choice(Computer will choose randomly not conditionally)
3.Result print

cases A:
    Rock - Rock = tie
    Rock - paper = paper win
    Rock - scissor = Rock win 

case B:
    paper - paper = tie
    paper - rock = paper win
    paper - scissor = scissor win

case C:
    scissor - scissor = tie 
    scissor - rock = rock win
    scissor - paper = scissor win


***************_3.Tic_Tac_Toe_Game_***************
Summary:
This is a GUI-based Tic Tac Toe game. The game is played on a $3 \times 3$ grid by two players: 
Player 1 and Player 2, using the symbols X and O. 
The first player to align three of their symbols horizontally, 
vertically, or diagonally wins the game!
